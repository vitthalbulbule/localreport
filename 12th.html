Educational Streams After 12th Grade

Science

  • Engineering: B.Tech/B.E. in fields like Computer Science, Mechanical, Civil, Electrical, etc.
  • Medical: MBBS, BDS, BAMS, BHMS, Nursing, Pharmacy, Biotechnology.
  • Pure Sciences: B.Sc. in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology, etc.
  • Research: Pursue B.Sc. and then M.Sc. or Ph.D. in specialized fields.
  • Architecture: B.Arch (requires Mathematics in 12th).
  • Defense Services: Join NDA, CDS, or other defense academies.
  • Aviation: Commercial Pilot Training, Aeronautical Engineering.

Commerce Stream

  • Chartered Accountancy (CA): A professional course in accounting and finance.
  • Company Secretary (CS): Focuses on corporate laws and governance.
  • Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com): General or specialized in Accounting, Finance, or Taxation.
  • Business Management: BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) or integrated MBA programs.
  • Economics: B.A. or B.Sc. in Economics.
  • Banking and Finance: Pursue certifications like CFA (Chartered Financial Analyst) or work in banking sectors.
  • Actuarial Science: A field combining mathematics, statistics, and finance.

Arts/Humanities Stream

  • Law: LLB (Bachelor of Laws) after completing any undergraduate degree.
  • Journalism and Mass Communication: B.A. in Journalism or Mass Communication.
  • Psychology: B.A. or B.Sc. in Psychology.
  • Social Work: B.A. in Social Work or Sociology.
  • Design: Fashion Design, Interior Design, Graphic Design, etc.
  • Teaching: B.Ed. after completing a bachelor’s degree.
  • Civil Services: Prepare for UPSC exams after graduation.
  • Performing Arts: Music, Dance, Theater, etc.

Vocational/Professional Courses

  • Diploma Courses: Engineering, IT, Hospitality, Animation, etc.
  • Hotel Management: BHM (Bachelor of Hotel Management).
  • Fashion Technology: B.F.Tech or diploma in Fashion Design.
  • Event Management: Diploma or certification courses.
  • Digital Marketing: Certification courses in SEO, SEM, Social Media Marketing, etc.
  • Animation and Multimedia: Diploma or degree courses in Animation, VFX, and Game Design.
