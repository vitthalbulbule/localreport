Importance of Computer Science

  • Automation: CS enables businesses to automate processes, reducing manual effort.
  • Data Science: It helps analyze vast amounts of data for decision-making.
  • Cybersecurity: CS professionals protect systems from digital threats.
  • AI & Machine Learning: CS drives advancements in AI, improving efficiency across industries.
