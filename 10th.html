Educational Streams After 10th Grade

Science

  • Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM): Ideal for careers in engineering, architecture, and physical sciences.
  • Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB): Suitable for careers in medicine, dentistry, pharmacy, and biological sciences.
  • PCM + Computer Science: For those interested in engineering and computer-related fields.

Commerse

  • Accountancy, Business Studies, and Economics: Best for careers in business, finance, accounting, and economics.
  • Commerce with Mathematics: For students considering careers in finance, economics, or actuary.

Arts/Humanities

  • History, Geography, Political Science, and Sociology: For careers in civil services, social work, education, and journalism.
  • Psychology, Economics, Literature: For those interested in arts, literature, and human behavior.

Vocational Courses

  • Diploma Courses: Various diploma courses are available in fields like engineering, hospitality, fashion designing, and more.
  • ITI Courses (Industrial Training Institute): Specialize in trades like electrician, mechanic, or plumber.
